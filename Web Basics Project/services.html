Commitment to Cultural Stewardship

For twenty five years I have worked internationally as a cultural anthropologist and design researcher working alongside design teams, developers, investors and marketers. I track and visualize how and why sensorial cues, media aesthetics and storytelling metaphors are evolving for Fortune 500 businesses across consumer technology, deep tech, media, gaming, transport, luxury, lifestyle and CPG sectors.

I have collaborated with Wolff Olins, Publicis, Futurebrand,Mother London, Fahrenheit 212 (Capgemini Invent), Space and Agency, Todo Design and Interbrand for a client roster that has included Google, Alphabet (X Development), Snap, Inc, Pixel, Samsung, Sony, IBM, DeBeers, Boucheron, Swarovski, CHANEL, Luxottica, Tapestry, Singapore Airlines, Virgin Airlines, BMW, LandRover, Fidelity, Pfizer, Procter & Gamble, Unilever, Pepsico, Coca-Cola, Starbucks, Clif Bar, Bragg's, Reuters, ABC, Virgin Media, Time Warner, Nike and Levis.

In 2023, I decided to pivot my professional engagement with cultural memory and image management to work directly with archives and special collections. I'm studying to attain the ALA certification within the History of Art and Design / Library and Information Science, MA/MS dual degree at Pratt Institute.
